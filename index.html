Foreign Worker Levy (FWL) Reference Tables

Monthly levy rates for Work Permit holders and S Pass holders by sector and skill tier

⚠️
The Foreign Worker Levy (FWL) applies only to Work Permit and S Pass holders. EP, PEP, ONE Pass, and EntrePass holders are exempt from levy. Levy must be paid by the 17th of each following month via GIRO. Late payment incurs penalties of 2% per month (min S$20, capped at 30%). The Daily levy rate = (monthly rate × 12) ÷ 365.

COMPASS Framework (Employment Pass)

Complementarity Assessment Framework — all new EP applications must score ≥ 40 points

ℹ️
COMPASS assesses EP applicants across 5 criteria — 4 foundational (up to 10 points each = 40 max) plus bonus criteria. Each criterion is rated Meets Expectations (10 pts), Below Expectations (0 pts), or Enhanced (20 pts). Applicants must accumulate at least 40 points to pass. Applies to new applications from 1 Sep 2023 and renewals from 1 Sep 2024.
C1
Salary Benchmark
Compared against local PMETs in same age range and industry sector. Must be at or above median to meet expectations; top one-third earns enhanced points.
C2
Qualifications
Based on educational attainment. Top-100 world universities, Singapore universities, and highly recognised institutions earn enhanced scores.
C3
Diversity of PMET Workforce
Firm-level criterion. Companies with diverse nationalities among PMETs score higher; over-reliance on one nationality is penalised.
C4
Local PMET Employment
Measures the firm's commitment to hiring locals. Companies with a strong proportion of local PMETs relative to industry peers earn higher scores.
C5 🌟
Skills Bonus (Shortage Occupation List)
Bonus points for roles on the 30-occupation Shortage Occupation List (SOL) covering ICT, financial services, healthcare, manufacturing and more for 2025.
C6 🌟
Strategic Economic Priority Bonus
Bonus points for firms in priority sectors or with strategic economic plans, e.g. Global Investor Programme, Startup SG, EdTech, CleanTech, etc.
