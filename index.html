COMPASS Framework (EP)

Complementarity Assessment — must score ≥ 40 points to pass

ℹ️
COMPASS has 4 foundational criteria (10 pts each) + 2 bonus criteria. Meets Expectations = 10 pts, Enhanced = 20 pts, Below = 0 pts. New applications from 1 Sep 2023; renewals from 1 Sep 2024.
C1
Salary Benchmark
Compared against local PMETs in same age and industry. Top one-third earns enhanced points.
C2
Qualifications
Top-100 world universities, Singapore universities, and highly recognised institutions score enhanced.
C3
Workforce Diversity
Firm's PMET nationality diversity — over-reliance on one nationality is penalised.
C4
Local PMET Employment
Firm's proportion of local PMETs vs industry peers — strong local hiring earns higher points.
C5 🌟
Skills Bonus (SOL)
Bonus for roles on the 30-occupation Shortage Occupation List — ICT, finance, healthcare, manufacturing.
C6 🌟
Strategic Priority Bonus
Firms in strategic sectors (GIP, Startup SG, CleanTech, EdTech, etc.) earn bonus points.
